<div style="float:left; position:relative;">
To add a button, left click on the buttons, then left click on the option you would like.
<ul>
	<li>Next page button - This button will move you forward 1 page</li>
	<li>Goto page button - This button will move you to the page you choose</li>
	<li>Button - generic button with many customisable features</li>
	<li>Score button - a button to help you build scoring quizzes</li>
</ul>
<br><br>
The cursor should now become a cross hair, and move over to the work area, then left click and start to draw the area you'd like to be the button.
