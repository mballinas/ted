<div style="float:left; position:relative;">
To add a rectangle to a page, left click on the text area option over on the right hand side. Then left click on insert  rectangle.
<br><br>
The cursor should now become a cross hair, and move over to the work area, then left click and start to draw the area you'd like to be the rectangle.
<br><br>
You can also use a rectangle to add background colour to an individual page.
<br><br>
You can see this as a video - <a target="new" href="helppages/insert_rectangle.swf">Adding a rectangle</a>
