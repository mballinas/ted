To delete an item from the workarea in TED there are two ways you can do it.
<ol>
	<li>Right click on the item in the work area and then left click on delete.</li>
	<li>Left click on the menu option Edit, then left click on delete.</li>
</ol>
Or you can see how to <a href="helppages/workarea_delete.swf" target="new">delete items in a work area</a> in a video.
