You can add a page in one of four ways
<ol>
	<li>Right click on a page and then left click on add - this will add a page at the end....</li>
	<li>.... or Right click on a page and then left click on add page here - this will add a page directly after the page you click on.</li>
	<li>On the pages menu at the top, select the add page option (actions as above)</li>
	<li>On the pages menu at the top, select the add page here option (actions as above)</li>
</ol>
Or you can see how to <a href="helppages/pages_add.swf" target="new">add pages</a> in a video.
