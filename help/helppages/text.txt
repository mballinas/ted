<div style="float:left; position:relative;">
To add some text to a page, left click on the text area option over on the right hand side. Then left click on insert text area.
<br><br>
The cursor should now become a cross hair, and move over to the work area, then left click and start to draw the area you'd like to be the text area.
<br><br>
To edit the text, double left click on the text area, then type in your text. Click outside the text area when you are done.
You can see this as a video - <a target="new" href="helppages/text_area.swf">Adding a text area</a>
